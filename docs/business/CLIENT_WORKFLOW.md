# Client Workflow

Use this before paid work, delivery, and hosting decisions. It is operational guidance, not legal advice.

## Proposal And Contract

- Confirm scope, pages/sections, timeline, price, VAT/tax handling, and revision rounds.
- Define who provides copy, photos, logos, menus/services, legal details, and approvals.
- Disclose the AI-assisted workflow.
- State that IP transfers after full payment.
- List third-party costs, privacy/cookie responsibilities, cancellation terms, liability limits, and signatures.

## Pricing Placeholders

- Audit/mockup: EUR [250-500].
- Starter static site: EUR [900-1500].
- Standard local site: EUR [1800-3500].
- Premium local presence: EUR [3500-7000+].
- Maintenance: EUR [79-250]/month.
- Managed hosting: EUR [25-100]/month.

## Delivery Checklist

- Website matches approved scope.
- All claims are source-backed or client-approved.
- Mobile layout is checked.
- Contact CTAs work.
- No unwanted analytics/tracking.
- Domain/DNS path is confirmed.
- Client accepts final version in writing.

## Hosting

Default to client-owned domain and hosting. Offer managed hosting only with clear monthly price, update limits, support window, backups, payment-failure process, and export policy.
